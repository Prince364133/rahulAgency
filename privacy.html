Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Data We Collect

When you fill out forms on our website, we collect the following information:

Information Provided by You:

  • Name — Your full name
  • Mobile Number — For order confirmation
  • City / Village — For delivery purposes
  • PIN Code — For delivery area verification

Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP Address — Your Internet Protocol address
  • Browser Type & Version — Type and version of your browser
  • Operating System / Platform — Your device's operating system
  • Screen Resolution — Your screen's resolution
  • Browser Language — Your browser's language setting
  • Timezone — Your time zone
  • Referring URL — The page from which you arrived
  • UTM Tracking Parameters — Advertising campaign tracking info (utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, utm_content)

2. How We Use Your Data

All collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Order processing and confirmation
  • Delivery coordination
  • Customer support
  • Ad campaign analysis (anonymous)

We do not sell your data to any third party.
